Welcome to to the Aim Game

Why the aim game?

Are you an aspiring programmer? Tired of mis-clicking your code and causing infinite loops? Well now there is a game that allows you to perfect your mouse/track pad skills. The aim game!

How to play

The player must try and click the dot before it moves to another positon on the screen. If you cannot click the dot in time you will get a strike! Three strikes and you are out!

As the game progresses, the dot will move faster and get smaller. Do your best to get your own personal highscore!
